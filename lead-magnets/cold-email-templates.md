## BEST PRACTICES

### Personalization Tips
- **Always** use their name in the greeting
- Reference their company or use case from signup note
- Mention specific pain points they shared
- Customize benefits to their situation

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep it under 50 characters
- Use their name or company when possible
- Avoid all caps and excessive punctuation
- A/B test different subject lines

### Timing Tips
- Send initial outreach within 24-48 hours of signup
- Follow up 48 hours after no reply
- Send break-up email after 3-4 follow-ups
- Time emails for Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Don't send generic templates without personalization
- Don't follow up too frequently (max once every 2-3 days)
- Don't use deceptive subject lines
- Don't forget to track opens and clicks
- Don't ignore replies - respond within 24 hours
