# Waitlist Scoring Checklist
### Identify which leads are 3x more likely to convert

Score your waitlist in 5 minutes using this simple framework. Focus on Hot leads first to hit 20%+ reply rates.

---

## 1. Domain Quality Check

**Score each lead's email domain:**

- [ ] **Company email domain** (e.g., john@company.com) → **+20 points**
- [ ] **Personal email** (gmail.com, outlook.com, etc.) → **+10 points**
- [ ] **Disposable/temporary email** (tempmail, 10minutemail, etc.) → **+2 points**
- [ ] **University/educational domain** (.edu) → **+15 points**

**Why it matters:** Company domains indicate professional interest. Educational domains often mean students or researchers who may not have budget.

---

## 2. Intent Signal Analysis

**Analyze the signup note for urgency and specificity:**

- [ ] **Urgent pain point** mentioned (e.g., "need now", "struggling with", "desperate for") → **+25-30 points**
- [ ] **Specific use case** described (e.g., "need for my SaaS", "for my e-commerce store") → **+15-25 points**
- [ ] **Vague interest** (e.g., "cool product", "interesting", "looks good") → **+5-10 points**
- [ ] **No signup note provided** → **+0 points**

**Why it matters:** Urgency and specificity are the strongest predictors of conversion. Someone with a specific problem is 3x more likely to buy than someone who just thinks it's "cool."

---

## 3. Recency Scoring

**Check when they signed up:**

- [ ] **Within 7 days** → **+20 points**
- [ ] **Within 30 days** → **+15 points**
- [ ] **Within 90 days** → **+10 points**
- [ ] **90+ days ago** → **+5 points**

**Why it matters:** Recent signups are still warm. The longer someone waits, the more likely they've found another solution or lost interest.

---

## 4. Source Quality

**Where did they come from?**

- [ ] **Referral from existing user** → **+20 points**
- [ ] **Product Hunt/launch platform** → **+15 points**
- [ ] **Social media** (Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.) → **+10 points**
- [ ] **Cold signup from website** → **+5 points**
- [ ] **Unknown source** → **+0 points**

**Why it matters:** Referrals and launch platforms indicate high intent. Cold website signups are often just browsing.

---

## 5. Calculate Total Score

**Add up all points for each lead:**

**Score range: 0-90**

| Segment | Score Range | Reply Rate |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| 🔥 **Hot** | 60-90 | 20%+ |
| 🌡️ **Warm** | 35-59 | 8-15% |
| ❄️ **Cold** | 0-34 | 2-5% |

---

## 6. Action Items

**For each lead:**

- [ ] Calculate total score (0-90)
- [ ] Segment: Hot (60+), Warm (35-59), Cold (<35)
- [ ] **Prioritize Hot leads** - reach out within 24 hours
- [ ] Create personalized emails for top 20 Hot leads
- [ ] Batch Warm leads for weekly outreach
- [ ] Put Cold leads on nurture sequence (monthly updates)

---

## Quick Reference: How to Personalize

**For Hot leads (60+ points):**
- Reference their specific pain point from signup note
- Mention their company/use case
- Offer a demo or trial immediately
- Follow up within 48 hours if no reply

**For Warm leads (35-59 points):**
- Ask about their timeline
- Share a relevant case study
- Offer educational content first
- Follow up weekly for 3 weeks

**For Cold leads (<35 points):**
- Add to newsletter for nurture
- Send monthly product updates
- Don't spend time on personalized outreach
- Re-score if they engage with nurture content
