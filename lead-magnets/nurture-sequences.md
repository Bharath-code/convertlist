## Tracking and Optimization

### Metrics to Track

**Email metrics:**
- Open rate (target: 30-50%)
- Click rate (target: 2-5%)
- Reply rate (target: varies by segment)
- Unsubscribe rate (target: <2%)

**Conversion metrics:**
- Lead magnet to trial (target: 5-10%)
- Trial to customer (target: 1-3%)
- Overall lead magnet ROI

### A/B Tests to Run

1. **Subject lines:** Benefit-focused vs. curiosity-driven
2. **Send time:** Morning vs. afternoon vs. evening
3. **Content length:** Short vs. long
4. **CTA placement:** Top vs. bottom vs. both
5. **Personalization:** With name vs. without

### Optimization Tips

- Send emails Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm
- Keep emails under 200 words
- One clear CTA per email
- Mobile-optimized (60% read on mobile)
- Test before sending to full list
