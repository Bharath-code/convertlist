## Giveaway Strategy

**Offer:** 6 months free to first 50 reviewers

**Why this works:**
- Incentivizes reviews (social proof)
- Gets users into the product
- 6 months = enough time to see value
- First 50 = urgency/scarcity

**Implementation:**
1. Create a coupon code in DodoPayments: `PH_LAUNCH_6MO` (6 months free)
2. In your first comment, mention the giveaway
3. Track who claims it
4. Follow up with them after 30 days to see if they converted

**Alternative if coupons aren't easy:** 
- Give 3 months free to anyone who comments
- DM them the code manually
- More work but builds relationships

## Launch Day Checklist

### Pre-Launch (1-2 days before)
- [ ] Create Product Hunt account if you don't have one
- [ ] Fill out all PH listing fields (logo, description, tags)
- [ ] Upload screenshots
- [ ] Record and upload demo video (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Prepare your first comment
- [ ] Create giveaway coupon code
- [ ] Test the product end-to-end (no bugs on launch day)
- [ ] Set up analytics tracking (UTM parameters for PH traffic)

### Launch Day
- [ ] Launch at 12:01 AM PT (Pacific Time) - PH resets at midnight
- [ ] Post your first comment immediately
- [ ] Share on Twitter/X with PH link
- [ ] Share on LinkedIn
- [ ] Share in Indie Hackers discord
- [ ] Share in relevant communities (IH, PH discord, etc.)
- [ ] Respond to every comment within 1 hour
- [ ] Upvote helpful comments from others
- [ ] Monitor for bugs/issues

### Post-Launch (24-48 hours)
- [ ] Continue responding to comments
- [ ] DM people who commented to thank them
- [ ] Send giveaway codes to reviewers
- [ ] Analyze traffic and signups
- [ ] Follow up with free tier users after 7 days

## Success Metrics

**Target:**
- 50-100 signups
- 5-10 paying users (mostly Launch $97 lifetime)
- Top 5 product of the day
- 50+ upvotes

**Good:**
- 25-50 signups
- 2-5 paying users
- Top 10 product
- 30+ upvotes

**Acceptable:**
- 10-25 signups
- 1-3 paying users
- Any placement
- 15+ upvotes

**What to do if it flops:**
- Don't panic. PH is hit-or-miss
- Cold email waitlist founders (task #7) - this is your real customer acquisition channel
- Content marketing (task #8)
- Launch again in 3-6 months with more features and social proof

## Follow-Up Strategy

**Day 1:** Thank everyone who commented, send giveaway codes

**Day 7:** Email free tier users who haven't upgraded:
"Hey, how's ConvertList working for you? Any questions? If you need more than 25 leads, upgrade to Pro for $29/mo or get the Launch deal for $97 lifetime."

**Day 30:** Email users who haven't converted:
"Quick check-in - have you converted any waitlist leads yet? Our data shows Hot leads convert 23% of the time vs 5% for Cold leads. Want to try scoring more leads?"

## Notes

- Product Hunt traffic is spike-based. Most signups happen in first 24 hours.
- Respond to EVERY comment. Even "cool product" - say "Thanks! Let me know if you have questions."
- Don't beg for upvotes. Focus on being helpful.
- The real customer acquisition channel is cold emailing waitlist founders (task #7), not PH. PH is for validation and initial traction.
