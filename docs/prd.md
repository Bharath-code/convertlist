# Waitlist Conversion Assistant — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview

Waitlist Conversion Assistant helps founders convert waitlist signups into early paying customers by prioritizing leads, generating outreach messages, and guiding conversion workflows.

This product is NOT a CRM.
This product is NOT an email sender.

It is a **conversion intelligence workflow tool** for pre-launch founders.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Founders collect waitlist emails but struggle to:

* know who is serious
* know what to say
* know how to convert interest into revenue
* avoid blasting entire waitlist blindly

Result:

* low conversion rates
* wasted time
* delayed revenue validation

---

## 3. Target Users (ICP)

Primary:

* Indie hackers launching SaaS
* AI tool founders
* Chrome extension creators
* solo bootstrapped founders

Secondary:

* early startup teams
* creator → product transitions

Typical waitlist size:

50 — 5000 leads.

---

## 4. Core Value Proposition

Help founders:

* prioritize conversations
* increase early conversion probability
* reduce launch anxiety
* follow a structured monetization workflow

---

## 5. MVP Scope

### 5.1 Lead Import

Users can:

* upload CSV
* paste email list

Fields supported:

* email
* signup_note (optional)
* source (optional)
* created_at (optional)

---

### 5.2 Segmentation Engine

System computes score:

* domain quality
* intent classification
* recency weighting
* source weighting

Outputs:

* Hot Leads
* Warm Leads
* Cold Leads

Each lead shows explanation.

---

### 5.3 Outreach Generator

User selects:

* individual lead OR segment

System generates:

* personalized outreach message
* CTA suggestion
* follow-up timing advice

---

### 5.4 Conversion Tracker

User manually marks:

* contacted
* replied
* paid

System updates lead confidence.

---

### 5.5 Dashboard

Shows:

* total leads
* segment distribution
* contacted count
* conversion progress

---

## 6. Success Metrics (MVP)
