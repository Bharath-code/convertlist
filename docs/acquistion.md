# First 20 Customers Acquisition Plan

## Step 1 — Twitter Hook

Post:

Waitlists don’t make money.

Conversions do.

Built a tool that shows which waitlist users will actually pay.

Giving early lifetime deal.

---

## Step 2 — Targeted DMs

Search tweets:

* "waitlist open"
* "launching soon"
* "building in public"

Message:

Hey — saw you’re launching soon.

I built a tool that helps convert waitlists into paying users.

Want early access? Offering lifetime deal.

Send 30–50 DMs daily.

---

## Step 3 — Indie Hackers Post

Title:

How I found my first paying users from a dead waitlist.

Tell story.
Soft-introduce product.

---

## Step 4 — Manual Onboarding

Talk to first users.

Ask:

* What surprised you?
* Who converted?
* What didn’t help?

Use feedback → improve scoring + messaging.
