# Cold Email Outreach Plan

## Strategy Overview

**Goal:** 10-25 users from cold emailing 500 waitlist founders (2-5% conversion)

**Why this works:** These are your exact ICP (ideal customer profile). They have waitlists, they're indie hackers, and they need to convert leads to customers.

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks to send all 500 emails

---

## Finding 500 Waitlist Founders

### Sources

**1. Product Hunt Upcoming**
- Go to producthunt.com/upcoming
- Filter for products with "waitlist" in description
- Find founder emails from:
  - Product Hunt profile
  - Website footer
  - LinkedIn
  - Twitter bio

**2. Recent Product Hunt Launches (last 3 months)**
- Search Product Hunt for "waitlist"
- Look for products that launched recently
- Find founder contact info

**3. Indie Hackers**
- Search IH for "waitlist"
- Look at launch posts
- Find founder emails from profiles

**4. Twitter/X**
- Search: "waitlist" AND "launching" AND "SaaS"
- Look for indie hackers with waitlists
- Find emails from their websites

**5. WaitlistKit/KickoffLabs customers**
- These tools have public customer lists sometimes
- Look for case studies or testimonials
- Find founder emails

### Target Criteria

**Ideal targets:**
- Indie hackers/SaaS founders
- Have 50-3000 leads on waitlist
- Recently launched or about to launch
- Technical founders (understand the value of AI scoring)

**Avoid:**
- Enterprise companies (wrong ICP)
- Non-SaaS businesses
- Products without waitlists

## Email Templates

### Template 1: Initial Outreach

**Subject:** Built a tool to convert waitlists

**Body:**
```
Hey [Name],

Saw you're building [Product Name] - looks great!

I built a tool called ConvertList that helps founders convert waitlist signups into paying customers.

The problem I kept seeing: Founders with 500-person waitlists who had no idea who to email first. They'd email everyone, get 2% replies, and give up.

ConvertList scores every lead by:
- Domain quality (company vs personal email)
- Intent from signup notes (urgent pain vs vague interest)  
- Recency (recent signups convert better)
- Source (referrals convert 3x better)

Then generates personalized outreach for each lead.

Want to try it free? I'll score your first 100 leads at no cost.

If it works, great. If not, no pressure.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Subject:** Re: waitlist conversion

I can score your first 100 leads free - takes 5 minutes to upload a CSV.

If the scoring is useful, we can talk about pricing. If not, no worries either way.

Let me know,
[Your Name]
```

```

Last check-in on this - happy to score your waitlist leads for free.

Our data shows Hot leads (scored 60+) convert 4-5x more than Cold leads.

If you're launching soon, knowing which leads to email first could make a big difference.

No pressure either way,

## Outreach Process

### Step 1: Build the List (Week 1)
- [ ] Find 500 waitlist founders using sources above
- [ ] Create spreadsheet with: Name, Email, Product Name, Product URL, Source
- [ ] Verify emails (use NeverBounce or similar to avoid bounces)
- [ ] Segment by quality (high priority = recent launch, clear waitlist, technical founder)

### Step 2: Send Initial Emails (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Send 50 emails/day (Monday-Friday)
- [ ] Personalize each email (mention their specific product)
- [ ] Track opens, clicks, replies in spreadsheet
- [ ] Use a tool like Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or Gmail with tracking

### Step 3: Follow-ups (Week 2-3)
- [ ] Send first follow-up to non-responders after 3 days
- [ ] Send second follow-up to non-responders after 7 days
- [ ] Respond to all replies within 24 hours

### Step 4: Handle Responses
- [ ] If they say yes: Send them a signup link with free 100-lead offer
- [ ] If they say no: Ask why (for learning)
- [ ] If they don't respond: Move to follow-up sequence

## Success Metrics

**Target:**
- 500 emails sent
- 50-100 responses (10-20%)
- 10-25 signups (2-5% of responses)
- 5-10 paying users

**Good:**
- 25-50 responses
- 5-10 signups
- 2-5 paying users

**Acceptable:**
- 10-25 responses
- 2-5 signups
- 1-2 paying users

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Personalize every email** - Mention their specific product, not generic "your product"
2. **Keep it short** - Founders are busy, get to the point
3. **No pressure** - Make it easy to say no
4. **Offer value first** - Free scoring before asking for money
5. **Respond fast** - Within 24 hours to all replies
6. **Track everything** - You'll learn what works
7. **Be genuine** - You're a solo dev too, relate to them

---

## What to Do If It Flops

If you get <5% response rate:

1. **Review your email** - Is it too long? Too generic?
2. **Check your list quality** - Are these actually your ICP?
3. **Try a different angle** - Focus on a specific pain point
4. **Warm up first** - Engage on Twitter/LinkedIn before emailing
5. **Pivot to other channels** - Content marketing, communities, etc.

## Follow-up After Sign-up

**Day 1:** Welcome email with getting started guide

**Day 3:** Check-in - "How's the scoring working? Any questions?"

**Day 7:** "Have you contacted any Hot leads yet? Our data shows they convert 23% of the time."

**Day 14:** If they haven't upgraded: "Need more than 25 leads? Upgrade to Pro for $29/mo or get the Launch deal for $97 lifetime."

**Day 30:** Final check-in before moving to non-active
